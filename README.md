 A project written in the Kotlin language as part of the development of an Android applications on the MAD course

## Rules
The rules of the game are quite simple: based on your preferences for word length and difficulty, a word is generated at random. Your task is to guess this word. As a hint, information about your previous attempts with the number of matching letters will be displayed. 

Different difficulty of the game depends on the number of attempts. The easy option has no limitations, medium has 15 attempts and the hard 7.

Have a fun!
